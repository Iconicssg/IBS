Service FAQs

How quickly can you start on a project?
We typically begin within 1–2 weeks of contract signing. For urgent requirements, we can mobilize our team within 48–72 hours depending on scope.
Do you work with small businesses?
Absolutely. We work with businesses of all sizes from SMEs to MNCs. Our Solution are scalable and priced to suit your specific needs and budget.
What industries do you specialize in?
We serve 15+ industries including fintech, banking, telco, retail, logistics, healthcare, hospitality, and technology across India and SEA.
How do you ensure recruitment quality?
We use a rigorous multi-stage screening process including competency-based interviews, psychometric assessments, and thorough reference checks to ensure only the best candidates are presented.
Are training programs delivered online or in-person?
We offer flexible delivery — fully in-person, fully virtual, or hybrid blended formats. All programs are customized to your team's needs and learning preferences.
Can we engage multiple services together?
Yes! Many of our clients engage us for multiple services simultaneously. Bundled engagements often come with cost advantages and better integration across projects.
